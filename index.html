    Student Testimonial

    I am from a nice and wealthy neighborhood in Seoul where there are multiple SAT prep academies with teachers who have excellent resumes. My parents and I decided to participate in a Summer Intensive course at one of these Hagwons in 2014. After attending numerous classes and solving SAT booklets, I saw no improvement and my score dwelled around 1700s. My counselor noticed that I needed to raise my SAT score up and recommended me the ThinkIVY Winter Camp. My score jumped by 200+ points in just 10 days by spending 8 hours per day. The mentors devoted their time and effort on me and my fellow camp participants. Now my mock test SAT score is around 2000, which I am looking forward to increase up to 2200+ by this summer.
